TechCorp Employee Policies and Benefits

Working Hours and Remote Work
Standard working hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM local time. However, TechCorp embraces flexible work arrangements. Employees can work remotely up to 3 days per week after completing their initial 90-day probationary period.

Vacation and Time Off
- Paid Time Off: 20 days per year for all full-time employees
- Sick Leave: 10 days per year
- Parental Leave: 16 weeks paid leave for primary caregivers, 8 weeks for secondary caregivers
- Public Holidays: All federal holidays are observed

Health and Wellness Benefits
TechCorp provides comprehensive health coverage including:
- Medical, dental, and vision insurance (100% premium covered for employees, 75% for dependents)
- Mental health support through our Employee Assistance Program
- Annual wellness stipend of $1,000 for gym memberships, fitness equipment, or wellness apps
- On-site yoga and meditation classes at headquarters

Professional Development
We invest in our employees' growth:
- Annual learning budget of $2,500 per employee
- Access to online learning platforms (Coursera, LinkedIn Learning, Udemy)
- Conference attendance support
- Internal mentorship program
- Quarterly lunch-and-learn sessions

Retirement and Financial Benefits
- 401(k) plan with 6% company match
- Employee Stock Purchase Plan (ESPP) with 15% discount
- Annual performance bonuses (up to 20% of base salary)
- Financial planning services

Equipment and Technology
All employees receive a laptop of their choice (Mac or Windows), plus a $500 budget for home office setup including desk, chair, monitor, and accessories.

Code of Conduct
All employees are expected to maintain professional standards, respect diversity and inclusion, and adhere to our ethics policy. Any violations should be reported to HR immediately.
